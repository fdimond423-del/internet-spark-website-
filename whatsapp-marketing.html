🛡️ Compliance & Best Practices

WhatsApp Marketing Done Right & Safely

Many businesses risk getting their numbers banned by using unethical bulk messaging methods. We ensure your WhatsApp marketing is 100% compliant with Meta's policies, protecting your business number and reputation.

  • Only send messages to contacts who have explicitly opted-in to receive communications
  • Use only Meta-approved message templates for outbound broadcasts
  • Always include an easy opt-out mechanism in every campaign
  • Respect messaging frequency limits and quiet hours
  • Use the official WhatsApp Business API (never unofficial tools)
  • Maintain a healthy "quality score" to protect message delivery
  • GDPR & DPDP Act compliant data handling practices
  • Regular list hygiene to remove inactive and opted-out numbers

❓ FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is bulk WhatsApp marketing legal in India? +
Yes — when done correctly. Bulk WhatsApp marketing is legal and compliant when using the official WhatsApp Business API and sending messages only to contacts who have opted in to receive communications from you. Using third-party bulk sending tools that bypass WhatsApp's systems is against Meta's Terms of Service and can result in number bans. We only use official, compliant methods.
What is the WhatsApp Business API and do I need it? +
The WhatsApp Business API is the enterprise-grade solution from Meta that enables businesses to send messages at scale, integrate chatbots, connect CRMs, and manage customer conversations through a team inbox. Unlike the WhatsApp Business App (which is limited to one device and basic features), the API enables full automation and scalability. If you want to send more than a few hundred messages per month or build automated workflows, the API is essential.
How much does WhatsApp Business API cost? +
WhatsApp Business API has a conversation-based pricing model set by Meta. In India, marketing conversations (business-initiated promotional messages) cost approximately ₹0.58–₹0.78 per conversation. Utility conversations (transactional messages like order updates) cost around ₹0.14 per conversation. Customer-initiated service conversations are free within a 24-hour window. Our management fees are separate and cover strategy, setup, creative, and optimization.
How do I build a WhatsApp subscriber list? +
The best methods include: Click-to-WhatsApp ads on Facebook/Instagram (highest quality leads), website opt-in forms with WhatsApp number field, QR codes in physical locations or packaging, social media CTAs, and importing your existing customer database (with their prior consent). We help you implement all these methods simultaneously for rapid, compliant list growth.
What types of content can I send via WhatsApp? +
WhatsApp supports rich media including text, images, videos, PDFs, documents, audio messages, interactive buttons (quick reply and call-to-action buttons), lists, and product catalogs. This makes it far more engaging than SMS or email. All promotional content must use pre-approved templates, while transactional and customer-service messages have more flexibility.
How quickly can we get started with WhatsApp marketing? +
For basic WhatsApp Business setup (without API), we can get you running within 1–2 days. For the full WhatsApp Business API setup, the process takes 3–7 business days including Meta verification, number registration, and chatbot configuration. We handle 100% of the technical setup — you just need to provide your business documents for verification. Your first campaign can go live within the first week.
