Our Story

From a Small Startup to an International Agency

Internet Spark was founded in 2017 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat with a simple but powerful vision: to help businesses harness the true power of digital marketing to achieve extraordinary growth.

What started as a small social media management firm with just 3 team members and a handful of clients has grown into a full-service international digital marketing powerhouse serving 500+ businesses across India, UAE, USA, UK, Canada, and Australia.

Over 8+ years, we have delivered 1000+ successful digital marketing projects, generated thousands of crores in revenue for our clients, and built a reputation as Ahmedabad's most trusted and results-focused digital marketing agency.
