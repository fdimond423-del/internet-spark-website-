❓ FAQ

Real Estate Marketing FAQs

What is a good CPL (Cost Per Lead) for real estate?
A good CPL varies by project type and location. For affordable housing (under ₹50L), target ₹200–400. For mid-range (₹50L–1.5Cr), ₹400–800. For luxury (above ₹1.5Cr), ₹1000–2500 is acceptable given higher margins. Our campaigns consistently outperform industry benchmarks.
How quickly can you start generating leads for a new project?
Once we have project details, visuals, and the landing page ready (which we create), we can launch campaigns within 7–10 days. Lead flow typically starts within 24 hours of campaign launch. We can also do a soft launch within 3–4 days for urgent projects.
Do you help with NRI real estate marketing?
Absolutely. We run targeted campaigns for NRI investors in UAE, USA, UK, Canada, and Australia. Meta and Google Ads can be geographically targeted to NRI-heavy cities. We also create NRI-specific landing pages and WhatsApp communication flows.
Do you work with individual real estate agents?
Yes! We have specific packages for individual agents focused on personal branding (LinkedIn + Instagram), listing promotion, and local area SEO. Pricing starts from ₹15,000/month for agents. Contact us for a customized agent marketing plan.
