❓ FAQ

LinkedIn Branding FAQs

Do I need to write the content or do you do it?
We handle all content creation. Our writers interview you (30-min monthly call) to understand your expertise and opinions, then craft posts that sound authentically like you. You review and approve everything before it goes live. You get a strong LinkedIn presence without spending hours writing.
How long does it take to see results on LinkedIn?
Initial profile view increases are usually visible within 2 weeks. Meaningful engagement and reach growth happens in 60–90 days. Lead generation from content typically starts in Month 3–4 as your audience builds trust. LinkedIn is a long-term play — but the results compound powerfully over time.
Will you post from my account?
We use a LinkedIn scheduling tool where we draft content and you approve it before publishing. You always have full control. Some clients share LinkedIn credentials for direct posting, but we recommend using approved scheduling tools for security.
Do you run LinkedIn Ads for B2B lead generation?
Yes! LinkedIn Lead Gen Form Ads are incredibly effective for B2B lead generation — targeting exact job titles, companies, industries, and seniority levels. We run Sponsored Content and InMail campaigns with precise B2B targeting. Minimum recommended LinkedIn Ads budget is ₹30,000/month.
