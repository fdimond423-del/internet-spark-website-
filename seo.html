SEO FAQs

How long does SEO take to show results?
Most websites see initial ranking improvements within 60–90 days. Significant traffic increases typically occur between months 3–6. Building to highly competitive positions can take 6–12 months. SEO is a long-term investment that pays compounding dividends for years.
Do you provide monthly SEO reports?
Yes. Every client receives a detailed monthly report showing keyword rankings, organic traffic trends, backlinks acquired, technical fixes implemented, and a clear explanation of what we did and why. You always know exactly what we're doing and what results it's producing.
Do you guarantee Google rankings?
No legitimate SEO agency can guarantee specific rankings since Google's algorithm has 200+ factors and changes frequently. However, we do guarantee our process: thorough keyword research, proper technical SEO, quality content creation, and ethical link building — the pillars that consistently produce results.
What is Local SEO and do you offer it?
Local SEO focuses on ranking your business for location-specific searches like "digital marketing agency in Ahmedabad." We specialize in local SEO for Gujarat businesses — including GMB optimization, local citations, and geo-targeted content that puts you in Google's Local Pack.
