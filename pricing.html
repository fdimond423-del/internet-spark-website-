FAQ

Pricing Questions Answered

Are there any setup fees or hidden charges?
No hidden fees whatsoever. The price you see is the price you pay. For paid ads (Google/Meta), ad spend is separate and goes directly to the platform — we don't mark it up. Our fee is purely for management, strategy, and creative services.
Can I switch plans or cancel at any time?
Yes. We recommend a minimum of 3 months for best results, but there's no forced lock-in. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel with 30 days' notice. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied in the first month.
Do you work with all business sizes?
Absolutely. From local shops to large enterprises, we have packages starting from ₹15,000/month. Our Starter plan is designed for small businesses just beginning digital marketing, while our Enterprise plan supports large-scale operations.
What's included in ad spend management?
We create, manage, and optimize your ad campaigns. The ad spend (money paid to Google/Meta) is not included in our fees — you pay that directly to the platforms. Our management fee covers strategy, creative, copywriting, targeting, optimization, and reporting.
Do you provide a custom quote for enterprise businesses?
Yes. For businesses with complex requirements, high ad budgets, or multiple locations/brands, we create completely custom proposals. Call us at +91 93137 02720 or email yashworkonly16@gmail.com for an enterprise consultation.
