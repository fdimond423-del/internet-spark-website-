❓ FAQ

Google Ads FAQs

What is PPC and how does Google Ads work?
PPC stands for Pay-Per-Click. In Google Ads, you bid on keywords related to your business. When users search those terms, your ad may appear. You only pay when someone clicks your ad. The position of your ad depends on your bid amount AND Quality Score (relevance + landing page quality).
What budget do I need for Google Ads?
Minimum effective budget varies by industry. We recommend ₹20,000–₹30,000/month ad spend for most local businesses. Competitive industries like real estate or legal may need ₹50,000+. Our fee is separate. We optimize every budget to maximize ROI regardless of size.
How quickly does Google Ads work?
Google Ads can drive traffic within hours of campaign launch. Unlike SEO which takes months, Google Ads is instant visibility. Most clients see solid results within the first 2 weeks, with performance improving as optimization data accumulates.
Do you manage Google Shopping Ads for e-commerce?
Absolutely. We set up and manage Google Merchant Center, create optimized product feeds, and run Shopping campaigns and Performance Max for e-commerce brands. Shopping Ads typically deliver 2X higher CTR than text ads for product searches.
What is a Google Partner agency?
Google Partners are agencies certified by Google that meet performance standards, ad spend thresholds, and maintain certifications. Being a Google Partner means we have access to Google's support, beta features, and training — which directly benefits our clients.
Can you handle Google Ads for local businesses?
Yes! Local campaigns are one of our strengths. We set up location targeting, call extensions, location extensions, and use Google Local Services Ads (where available) to drive calls and walk-ins for local businesses including restaurants, clinics, gyms, salons, and retailers.
