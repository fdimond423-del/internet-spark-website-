What is Lead Generation?

Turn Strangers Into Paying Customers

Lead generation is the process of attracting and converting strangers and prospects into someone who has indicated interest in your company's product or service. It is the lifeblood of any growing business — without a consistent flow of new leads, revenue growth stagnates and businesses plateau.

For B2C businesses, lead generation means capturing contact details from potential buyers using compelling offers, contests, free trials, or product demonstrations — then nurturing them through targeted messaging until they're ready to buy.

For B2B businesses, lead generation is about identifying decision-makers, building trust over time through educational content and remarketing, and ultimately booking meetings or demos that convert into contracts worth lakhs or crores.

At Internet Spark, we design full-funnel lead generation ecosystems — not just ads. We build the complete infrastructure: lead magnets, landing pages, CRM pipelines, email sequences, and retargeting campaigns — so your business generates leads on autopilot 24/7.

Our Process

The 7-Step Lead Gen System That Works

We follow a proven, repeatable process that transforms your marketing from guesswork into a predictable, scalable revenue engine. Every campaign is built on data, tested rigorously, and optimized continuously.

Start Your Campaign
1

🧩 Define Your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

We start with deep research to build a crystal-clear picture of your ideal buyer — demographics, psychographics, pain points, objections, and buying triggers. This foundation ensures every rupee of ad spend is laser-targeted at people most likely to buy.

2

🎁 Create an Irresistible Offer

We design your lead magnet and core offer — the value exchange that makes prospects willing to give you their contact information. This could be a free consultation, a discount, a downloadable guide, or a free audit. The offer must be specific, valuable, and instantly desirable.

3

🏗️ Build Your Lead Generation Funnel

We architect the complete funnel: awareness → interest → consideration → action. This includes landing pages, thank-you pages, lead forms, confirmation emails, and initial nurture sequences. Every element is designed to minimize friction and maximize conversions.

4

🚦 Drive Targeted Traffic

We launch paid campaigns across Facebook, Google, LinkedIn, and/or WhatsApp — targeting your ICP with precision. We A/B test multiple ad creatives and audiences simultaneously to rapidly identify what works best, then scale winning combinations aggressively.

5

📥 Capture & Qualify Leads

Leads are captured through optimized forms, WhatsApp chats, or phone calls. We use qualifying questions and lead scoring to immediately segment hot, warm, and cold leads — so your team knows exactly who to call first and how to approach each conversation.

6

🌡️ Nurture with Automated Sequences

Automated email and WhatsApp sequences begin immediately, delivering value, answering FAQs, sharing testimonials, and handling common objections. Leads are kept warm and engaged until they're ready to buy — even if that's 3 weeks after their first touchpoint.

7

💰 Convert & Optimize Continuously

We track conversion rates at every stage of the funnel and continuously optimize — testing new ad copy, landing page variants, email subject lines, and offers. Each optimization cycle improves CPL and conversion rates, making your lead gen machine more profitable over time.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Lead Generation

How quickly will I start seeing leads after the campaign launches?
For paid channels like Facebook Ads and Google Ads, you can typically expect your first leads within 24–72 hours of campaign launch. However, for the first 7–14 days, we're in the optimization phase — testing different audiences, creatives, and offers. Lead volume and quality ramp up significantly after this initial learning period. For SEO-based organic lead generation, results typically begin appearing in 3–6 months. We generally recommend starting with paid channels for immediate results while building your organic presence simultaneously.
What is a realistic Cost Per Lead (CPL) I can expect?
CPL varies significantly by industry, target audience, channel, and offer quality. Here are typical ranges we achieve: Real Estate: ₹150–₹500/lead | Healthcare: ₹80–₹300/lead | Education: ₹20–₹100/lead | Finance/Insurance: ₹80–₹250/lead | B2B/SaaS: ₹500–₹2,500/lead. These are significantly lower than industry averages because of our optimization expertise. During the discovery call, we'll give you a specific CPL estimate for your industry and setup after reviewing your goals and budget.
What CRMs do you integrate with, and how does the lead flow work?
We integrate with all major CRMs including HubSpot, Zoho CRM, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Freshsales, Monday.com, and Notion. We also build custom integrations via Zapier or direct API. When a lead submits a form or clicks your WhatsApp button, their data is automatically captured and pushed to your CRM within seconds — complete with source tracking, UTM parameters, and any qualifying answers they provided. Your sales team receives an instant notification via email or Slack and can act within minutes of the lead coming in.
How do you ensure lead quality — not just quantity?
Lead quality is something we take extremely seriously. We improve quality through multiple mechanisms: (1) Qualification questions in lead forms that filter out non-serious prospects; (2) Lead scoring models that rank leads based on engagement, fit, and intent signals; (3) Specific targeting parameters that match your ICP; (4) Compelling but specific offers that attract genuinely interested people (not freebie hunters); (5) Regular feedback loops with your sales team — we review conversion data weekly and continuously refine targeting and messaging based on which types of leads actually close into customers.
What's the minimum ad budget required for lead generation campaigns?
We recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹15,000–₹20,000/month for Facebook or Instagram lead generation campaigns. For Google Ads, the minimum is ₹20,000–₹30,000/month due to higher CPCs in competitive industries. For LinkedIn Ads, budget starts at ₹40,000/month given the higher CPC environment. These are ad spends, separate from our management fee. That said, we've achieved exceptional results for clients starting with smaller budgets by focusing spend on the highest-converting single channel first, then expanding as the campaign proves profitable.
Do you also handle the follow-up and nurturing after leads come in?
Yes! Lead nurturing is a critical part of our service — because 73% of leads are not ready to buy immediately. We build automated nurture sequences via email and/or WhatsApp that: send an instant welcome message with a reminder of what they signed up for, deliver educational content that builds trust and desire over 7–21 days, share social proof like testimonials and case studies, address common objections proactively, and prompt them to book a call or make a purchase when their engagement signals indicate readiness. This automated follow-up dramatically improves the percentage of leads that eventually convert to customers.
