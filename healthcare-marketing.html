❓ FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from doctors, clinics, and hospitals about our healthcare digital marketing services.

Is digital marketing allowed for doctors in India?
Yes, digital marketing is allowed for healthcare providers in India, but it must comply with the National Medical Commission (NMC) code of ethics and Indian Medical Council (Professional Conduct, Etiquette and Ethics) Regulations, 2002. This means avoiding exaggerated claims, prohibited testimonials, and guarantee-of-cure language. Internet Spark specializes in creating highly effective healthcare marketing campaigns that are 100% compliant with all applicable regulations — so you attract more patients without any risk to your medical license or professional reputation.
How long does it take to see results from healthcare marketing?
Results depend on the channels used. Google Ads typically start generating patient inquiries within 1–2 weeks of launch. Google My Business optimization can show increased calls and visits within 2–4 weeks. SEO is a longer-term strategy that typically shows significant improvement in 3–6 months, but delivers the most cost-effective and sustainable patient flow. Social media builds consistent community engagement over 2–3 months. Most of our healthcare clients see a meaningful increase in patient inquiries within the first 30–60 days of our engagement.
Can you help with Practo and JustDial profiles as well?
Absolutely. We optimize your profiles on all major healthcare platforms including Practo, Lybrate, JustDial, Sulekha, and UrbanClap/Urban Company (for wellness practitioners). We ensure your profiles are fully filled, use the right keywords, have compelling descriptions, and are linked to your website. We also help you manage and respond to reviews on these platforms. A comprehensive multi-platform presence significantly increases your chances of being found by patients wherever they search.
Do you work with single doctors or only hospitals?
We work with the entire spectrum of healthcare providers — from individual solo practitioners (general physicians, dentists, dermatologists, etc.) to small clinics, multi-doctor setups, diagnostic centers, and large multi-specialty hospitals. Each engagement is custom-sized to your practice's current patient volume, marketing goals, and budget. A solo doctor typically needs a different marketing approach than a 100-bed hospital, and we tailor our strategy accordingly.
How do you handle before/after photos or patient testimonials?
We handle patient testimonials and case study content with extreme care and a strict ethics protocol. Before using any patient image, video, or testimonial, we obtain explicit written consent from the patient clearly stating what content will be used, where it will be published, and for how long. We also ensure the content cannot be linked back to the patient if they wish to remain anonymous. For before/after images (common in dermatology and dental practices), we follow platform-specific guidelines to ensure compliance. Privacy and dignity of patients are non-negotiable in our content creation process.
What makes your healthcare marketing different from a general agency?
Healthcare marketing requires specialized knowledge that generic digital agencies simply don't have. Our team understands medical terminology, patient psychology, compliance requirements, and the sensitivity of healthcare content. We know that a patient searching for "cancer treatment" is in a very different emotional state than someone searching for "new smartphone" — and our content and ad copy reflects that understanding. We also have dedicated experience with healthcare-specific platforms (Practo, Lybrate), healthcare Google Ad policies, and the unique trust-building requirements of the medical industry. This specialization delivers significantly better patient acquisition results compared to working with a generic agency.
