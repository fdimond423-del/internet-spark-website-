FAQ

Social Media Marketing FAQs

How many posts do you create per month?
Depending on your package, we create 12–40+ posts per month per platform, including static posts, carousels, reels, and stories. All content is planned in advance with a monthly content calendar you approve.
Which social media platforms should my business be on?
It depends on your target audience and business type. B2C businesses typically benefit most from Instagram and Facebook. B2B companies should prioritize LinkedIn. E-commerce brands do well on Instagram + Pinterest. We recommend platforms based on where your customers spend time.
How long before I see results from social media marketing?
Organic growth typically shows meaningful results in 60–90 days. Engagement improvements are usually visible within 30 days. Paid social campaigns can show results immediately. We set realistic milestone expectations during onboarding.
Do you handle reels and video content?
Yes! Our Growth and Scale plans include professional reel creation. We conceptualize, script, edit, and post reels optimized for maximum reach on Instagram and Facebook. Video content is the highest-performing format on social media.
Will I have approval over content before it's posted?
Absolutely. We share a full monthly content calendar for your review and approval before any content is scheduled. You can request revisions and we'll adjust until you're 100% satisfied.
Do you manage social media ads as part of this service?
Yes, our Growth and Scale packages include paid social media advertising. Ad spend is separate (paid directly to Meta/Google), and we manage the campaigns, creative, targeting, and optimization as part of our service.
