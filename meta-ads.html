FAQ

Meta Ads FAQs

How much should I spend on Meta Ads?
We recommend a minimum budget of ₹15,000–₹20,000/month to generate meaningful data and results. Our management fee is separate from ad spend. For lead generation, ₹30,000–₹50,000/month gives excellent results. We optimize every rupee for maximum impact.
How soon will I see results from Meta Ads?
Meta Ads can start delivering results within 24–48 hours of launch. However, the first 2 weeks is the "learning phase" where the algorithm optimizes delivery. Typically, you'll see strong performance from Week 3 onwards as optimization kicks in.
What is ROAS and what ROAS can I expect?
ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) measures revenue generated per rupee spent on ads. If you spend ₹1 lakh and generate ₹4 lakh in sales, that's 4X ROAS. Our average ROAS across all clients is 4.2X, with many e-commerce clients achieving 6X+ during optimized campaigns.
Do you create the ad creatives or do I provide them?
We create all ad creatives as part of our service — images, videos, carousels, and reels. Our design team produces platform-optimized creatives tested for maximum CTR and conversions. You approve all creatives before they go live.
What happens to my ad account and data if I stop?
Your ad account always remains yours — we work within your Business Manager. If you ever stop, you retain full ownership of your account, all campaign data, custom audiences, and pixel data. We never hold client accounts hostage.
Do you run WhatsApp Click-to-Chat ads?
Yes! Click-to-WhatsApp ads are one of our specialties for Indian businesses. They drive users directly to a WhatsApp conversation, bypassing forms and dramatically increasing conversion rates. Perfect for real estate, healthcare, education, and service businesses.
