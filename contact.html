FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?
Results vary by service. Google Ads and Meta Ads can show results within days. SEO typically takes 60–90 days to show significant movement. Social media growth usually becomes visible within 30–60 days. We set realistic expectations during our onboarding call.
What is your minimum contract period?
We typically recommend a minimum of 3 months to see measurable results, especially for SEO and social media. For paid ads, we can start with a month-to-month arrangement. However, our best results come from long-term partnerships of 6–12+ months.
Do you work with businesses outside Ahmedabad?
Absolutely! While we're headquartered in Ahmedabad, we serve clients across India and internationally in UAE, USA, UK, Canada, and Australia. All our services are delivered remotely with regular video call check-ins.
What makes Internet Spark different from other agencies?
We're 100% results-focused. Every strategy is backed by data, every campaign is A/B tested, and every report shows exactly what's working and why. You get a dedicated account manager, full transparency, and a team that genuinely cares about your growth — not just retaining your contract.
Can I get a free consultation before committing?
Yes! We offer a completely free 45-minute strategy session where we audit your current digital presence, identify growth opportunities, and present a custom roadmap — with no obligation to sign up. Fill in the form above to book yours.
