🏪 Marketplace vs Own Website

Smart E-commerce Strategy: Where Should You Sell?

One of the most important strategic decisions for online sellers. We help you understand the trade-offs and build a blended approach that maximizes revenue.

🏪 Marketplace Strategy (Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho)

  • Instant access to millions of buyers
  • Lower entry barrier & setup costs
  • Built-in trust & fulfillment infrastructure
  • Sponsored product ads for visibility
  • A+ content and brand store creation
  • Review management & rating optimization
  • Best for: New brands, commodity products, testing SKUs

🌐 Own Website Strategy (Shopify, WooCommerce)

  • Full control over brand experience
  • Higher profit margins (no marketplace fees)
  • Build your own customer database
  • Unlimited customization & branding
  • Own email list & retargeting audience
  • SEO-driven long-term organic traffic
  • Best for: Established brands, premium products, D2C growth

💡 Our Recommendation: A blended strategy works best. Leverage marketplaces for volume and brand discovery, while building your own website for margin, loyalty, and long-term brand equity. We help you execute both simultaneously.

❓ FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How much ad budget do I need to start e-commerce marketing? +
We recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹15,000–₹30,000/month to generate enough data for optimization. However, the ideal budget depends on your product price point, competition, and goals. For brands wanting to scale aggressively, ₹1L+/month in ad spend is where we see the most significant results. Our management fee is separate from your ad spend budget.
How long does it take to see results from e-commerce marketing? +
With paid advertising (Google & Meta Shopping), you can start seeing traffic and sales within 1–2 weeks. However, campaign optimization typically takes 4–8 weeks as we gather data and refine targeting. Email automation shows results within 2–4 weeks. SEO takes 3–6 months for significant organic traffic growth. We set realistic expectations and show you progress metrics from day one.
What is ROAS and what ROAS can I realistically expect? +
ROAS stands for Return on Ad Spend. A 4x ROAS means for every ₹1 you spend on advertising, you generate ₹4 in revenue. Our client average is 4.2x ROAS, though results vary by product category, price point, and margins. Fashion and beauty brands often achieve 5–8x ROAS with retargeting, while new product launches may start at 2–3x ROAS as we build audiences and optimize.
Do you work with businesses selling on Amazon and Flipkart? +
Absolutely! We manage Sponsored Product Ads, Sponsored Brand Ads, and Sponsored Display Ads on Amazon. For Flipkart, we handle FlipkartAds campaigns, product listing optimization, and A+ content creation. We also work with Meesho sellers and ONDC-registered businesses. We can manage both your marketplace presence and own website simultaneously for maximum revenue.
What's the difference between you and a regular digital marketing agency? +
Most digital marketing agencies treat e-commerce like any other business. We specialize in the unique challenges of online stores — product feed management, catalogue advertising, cart abandonment recovery, seasonal campaign planning, LTV optimization, and marketplace-specific strategies. Our team has hands-on experience running e-commerce businesses, not just marketing them.
Can you help with a product launch on a new Shopify store? +
Yes! We love working with new launches. Our launch strategy includes pre-launch buzz building on social media, influencer seeding, an email/SMS waitlist campaign, a launch day paid advertising blitz, and post-launch retention marketing to maximize repeat purchases. We can also review your store's UX and conversion rate optimization before launch to ensure every visitor counts.
